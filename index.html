I started my career as a solutions engineer, diving deep into the technical details of complex enterprise systems. What I discovered was that my true passion wasn't just building great technology—it was helping organizations understand how technology could fundamentally transform their business.

Over the past 15 years, I've evolved from individual contributor to technical sales leader, helping enterprise organizations build the teams, processes, and partnerships that turn technical innovation into sustainable revenue growth.
